## üö® Disaster Alerts

Stay informed about natural disasters and emergencies in your area.

### How to Use

1. Visit the **Alerts** page from the navigation menu
2. Click **"Manage Subscription"** to customize your preferences
3. Select the districts you want to monitor
4. Choose alert types (flood, cyclone, earthquake, landslide, fire)
5. Click **"Save Subscription"**

Active alerts will display automatically on the Alerts page with:
- Severity level (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
- Affected districts and areas
- Alert message in English and Tamil
- Expiration time

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does the app work offline?**
A: Limited functionality. SOS requests will be sent via SMS when offline. Tracking requires internet.

**Q: How accurate is the ambulance location?**
A: Depends on driver's GPS. Typically accurate within 10-50 meters.

**Q: Can I cancel an emergency request?**
A: Yes, contact the driver immediately or call 108 dispatch.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use encryption and follow data privacy standards.

**Q: How do I become a blood donor?**
A: Visit the Blood Donors page and click "Register as Donor".
